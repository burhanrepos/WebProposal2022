  1. Introduction

    2. Multi-Tasker will be a reliable platform that connects users who call to deploy tasks and find regional services, with tradesmen who are looking to earn money and ready to work.

  2. Objectives

    3. The Objective of this system is to provide easy assistance to both users and tradesmen by connecting the two individuals with a proper UI, database, and information.

  3. Problem Description

    4. To create a platform where all service providers are one click away from the users. While the rest of the world has already made the shift to the online platform, there is a need for the daily services to be shifted to the online platform too. As the tradesmen also want the least possible idle time in between their services, this solution will be providing the tradesmen with their next job without any hassle or waiting for their next job.

  4. Methodology

      5. Following Methodologies will be used forwebsitedevelopment:

      Front-End: HTML/CSS/JavaScript/React

      Back-End: NodeJS / Django

      API’s: According to needs API’s will be used

  5. Project Scope

    6. The Multi-Tasker framework acts as a mediator between the end-user and the services supplier (Tradesmen) and fills the holes of communication utilizing the most recent innovation. This framework moreover takes out the “guesswork” and ambiguity included within the field, for example guessing which individual is right for a specific job.

  1. Brief Feasibility Study

    2. Yes, we will meet our project schedule according to Project Scope defined above

    i. Risks Involved : As the website provides access to a physical service, there are risks of a physical injury to the tradesmen. Therefore, the users will sign a disclaimer where they will declare that they would not knowingly or unknowingly put the tradesmen in a situation where they are in physical danger.

    ii. Resource Requirement : System permissions would be required to access different resources on the local device such as, location, phone, internet services etc

  2. Solution Application Areas

    3. The proposed solution will be applied in daily households wherever there are any opportunities of maintenance and chores around the house. Thus, the public living in any style of house will be the user of the website. Meanwhile on the other end, the users of the website will be tradesmen who are looking for chores around their area and looking to reduce their idle time.

  3. Tools/Technology

    4. Following tools/Technologies will be used for Website development:

    i. Technologies : HTML/CSS/JavaScript/React, NodeJS / Django, MySQL / MongoDB, according to needs API’s will be used

    ii. Tools : VS Code, PyCharm, WAMP/XAMPP Server, PhpMyAdmin
